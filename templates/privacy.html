Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

Imagine Shelter was created as a quiet place where people can share their thoughts without fear of judgement. Protecting your privacy is one of our highest priorities.

Anonymous Stories

Stories shared through Imagine Shelter are anonymous. We do not ask for your name or require an account to submit a story.

Listening Sessions

When you book a listening session, payments are securely processed through Razorpay. Imagine Shelter never stores your card or banking details.

Information We Store

  • Anonymous stories you choose to share.
  • Booking details needed to reserve your session.
  • Basic technical information required for the website to function.

Information We Don't Sell

We never sell your anonymous stories or personal information to advertisers or third parties.

Your Responsibility

Please avoid sharing passwords, banking information, government IDs, or other highly sensitive personal details in your stories.
